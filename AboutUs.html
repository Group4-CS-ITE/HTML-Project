Our Story

Sizzla began with a shared dream among four passionate individuals to bring the warmth and authenticity of Filipino cuisine straight to every Filipino’s doorstep. What started as a humble local restaurant soon grew into a nationwide sensation, fueled by dedication, innovation, and a deep love for Filipino food.

At Sizzla, every dish is a celebration of our country’s rich culinary heritage. Our thoughtfully crafted menu showcases the perfect balance of savory, sweet, sour, and spicy flavors that define Filipino cooking. From well-loved favorites like lumpia, crispy pata, bistek, and sisig, to comforting rice options like plain or sinangag, every bite captures the essence of home.

To complete the dining experience, Sizzla offers a delightful selection of Filipino desserts from the colorful and refreshing halo-halo, to the classic buko pie, and a variety of smooth, creamy ice creams that sweeten every meal.

Today, Sizzla stands as more than just a restaurant. It is a movement that connects Filipinos through food. A trusted name that brings the familiar flavors of home to every community across the nation. From our kitchen to your table, we continue to serve the taste of tradition, one delivery at a time.
